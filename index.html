Titi Akinsanmi is a leading voice in public and product policy, Titi Akinṣanmi shapes the digital economy with a focus on fostering innovation. Her global experience spans the public and private sectors, academia, and international institutions. As Google’s Global AI Policy Team Lead, Titi spearheads policy development for responsible AI. She has also held regional government affairs and public policy roles, engaging with policymakers on critical issues like privacy, data governance, and internet policy within Google. Titi’s expertise lies in building bridges between governments, the private sector, and civil society. She helps these stakeholders understand how public policy can harness digital opportunities while mitigating risks and fostering collaboration. A thought leader with in-depth global experience on technology and its adoption, Titi’s insights are sought after by leading organizations and governments.
